Mr. Emmanuel Agada

The Man With The Golden Heart

Here is a time line of Mr. Emmanuel Agada's life:

  • 1965 - Born in Markurdi, Benue State
  • 1985 - Leaves the village to Kaduna State.
  • 1988 - Started Schooling in Kaduna State Polytechnic
  • 1995 - A graduate. Working in a printing Press.
  • 1996 - Found the Love of his Life in Kaduna State.
  • 1997 - Got married to the love og his life and relocated to Abuja. Started his own printing press
  • 1998 - Had a baby girl !!! He named after his mother-in-law
  • 2000 - Got a Federal Government Job. Was running his business by the side too
  • 2001 - Had a baby Boy. His second child
  • 2003 - Fell ill and died.

"Any thing worth doing is what doing well"
